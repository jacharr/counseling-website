About Us

Jaxx Waxx only uses all natural, sustainable ingredients. We use soy wax and therapeutic grade essential oils for fragrance. Paraffin candles and even soy wax candles with synthetic fragrances have been shown to have negative health effects, and Jaxx Waxx was founded to give people candles that protect their health and even enhance it with the positive effects of essential oils.
